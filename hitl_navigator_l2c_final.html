HITL Design Insight — L2C

L2C carries the highest Knowledge concentration of any module — 7 steps at 65% H reflect the relationship-driven nature of marketing and sales. 8 Transaction steps (35% H) are AI absorption targets: lead generation, campaign execution, channel execution, and order management. 4 Decision gates (80% H) carry revenue and customer authority — irreducibly human. L2C aggregate at M3: 57% Human / 43% AI. Flow terminates with handoff to Order-to-Cash at PCF 3.5.4.
