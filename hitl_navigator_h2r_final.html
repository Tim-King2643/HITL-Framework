HITL Design Insight — H2R

2 Decision gates (80% Human at M3) anchor the irreducibly human layer — candidate selection and offer approval carry legal and strategic authority. 10 Knowledge steps (65% Human at M3) span strategy, performance, development, and advisory work where human judgment is the primary value driver. 17 Transaction steps (35% Human at M3) represent the AI absorption core — HRIS automation, payroll processing, access provisioning, and benefits administration. 2 Exception flows (82% Human) cover employee relations and separation — sensitive, high-judgment work. H2R aggregate at M3: 62% Human / 38% AI — reflecting a fundamentally human-centric lifecycle process with high AI opportunity in transactional administration.
