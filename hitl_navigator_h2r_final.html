HITL Design Insight — H2R

7 Decision gates (80% Human) are the irreducibly human layer — hiring, performance, and career decisions carry people authority that must remain human by design. 6 Knowledge steps (65% Human) represent the AI-augmented opportunity — sourcing, onboarding, training, and workforce analytics. 2 Exception flows (82% Human) require human resolution. H2R aggregate at M3: 63% Human / 37% AI — reflecting the judgment-heavy nature of the HR lifecycle.
