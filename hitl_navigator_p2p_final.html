HITL Design Insight — P2P

16 Transaction-pattern steps (35% Human at M3) represent the AI absorption opportunity — receiving, posting, issuing, and releasing in rules-based sequence. 4 Decision gates (80% Human) are the irreducibly human layer — accept/reject judgments on POs, receipts, invoices, and payment approval. These steps carry financial authority that must remain human by design. 5 Exception flows (82% Human) require human resolution. P2P aggregate at M3: 53% Human / 47% AI — consistent with the enterprise-wide 18-point gap finding.
