HITL Design Insight — P2P

5 Decision gates (80% Human at M3) are the irreducibly human layer — supplier selection, requisition approval, and payment authorization carry strategic and financial authority that must remain human by design. 13 Transaction-pattern steps (35% Human at M3) represent the AI absorption opportunity — receive, create, distribute, and post steps in rules-based sequence. 4 Exception flows (82% Human) require human resolution. P2P aggregate at M3: 55% Human / 45% AI — grounded in APQC PCF SIPOC reference architecture and consistent with the enterprise-wide Human-AI convergence finding.
