HITL Design Insight — O2C

4 Decision gates (80% Human at M3) are the irreducibly human layer — order acceptance, availability determination, and fulfillment routing carry commercial and customer authority that must remain human by design. 12 Transaction-pattern steps (35% Human at M3) represent the AI absorption opportunity — fulfillment, invoicing, AR maintenance, payment processing, and cash application in rules-based sequence. 4 Exception flows (82% Human) require human resolution — backorders, order inquiries, collections/dunning, and dispute resolution. O2C aggregate at M3: 52% Human / 48% AI — grounded in APQC PCF SIPOC reference architecture and consistent with the enterprise-wide Human-AI convergence finding.
