HITL Design Insight — O2C

13 Transaction-pattern steps (35% Human at M3) represent the AI absorption opportunity — order intake, pick/pack/ship, invoice transmission, and payment posting. 2 Decision gates (80% Human) are the irreducibly human layer — Validate Order and Accept/Reject Payment carry revenue authority that must remain human by design. 4 Exception flows (82% Human) require human resolution. O2C aggregate at M3: 52% Human / 48% AI — consistent with the enterprise-wide convergence pattern.
