HITL Design Insight — P2Pr

P2Pr has the largest AI convergence gap at -25% — driven by high transaction volume across production execution, work order management, materials receiving, and inventory management. Engineering anchors the process with BOMs, routings, and ECOs — the irreducible human judgment that defines what gets made and how. Materials is the operational hub: planning, inventory, logistics, and the handoff to Purchasing (P2P at PCF 4.2). Manufacturing executes, inspects, and closes the loop back to Materials. P2Pr aggregate at M3: 53% Human / 47% AI.
