HITL Design Insight — S2E

8 Decision gates (80% Human at M3) anchor the irreducibly human layer — vision-setting, mission, enterprise strategy, organizational design, and initiative selection carry authority that cannot be delegated to AI. 12 Knowledge steps (65% Human at M3) span environmental scanning, options analysis, business unit strategy, and execution oversight — the largest cluster in the process, and where AI augmentation (not replacement) of research and synthesis work offers the clearest opportunity. 2 Document steps (40% Human at M3) cover metrics definition and strategy communication. Unlike P2P, O2C, and H2R, S2E has no Transaction or Exception steps — strategy formation has no routine-transactional core to absorb into AI, which is why the convergence gap is the smallest in the suite. S2E aggregate at M3: 68% Human / 32% AI — reflecting a fundamentally judgment-driven process where AI's role is augmenting human strategic thinking, not replacing decision authority.
