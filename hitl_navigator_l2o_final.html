HITL Design Insight — L2O

Manage Leads/Opportunities (3.5.1, 65% Human at M3) covers the full sales-pipeline arc at the process level — identifying and qualifying prospects, matching opportunities to strategy, developing win plans, managing pipeline progression, allocating sales resources, and running the customer sales-call cycle through to close. Classified as a Knowledge process: the work is dominated by judgment, synthesis, and relationship-building rather than routine transactions, even though qualification and resource-allocation decisions sit inside it. Develop & Manage Sales Proposals, Bids & Quotes (3.5.3, 60% Human at M3) covers the RFP-to-outcome arc — receiving and refining requirements, assessing competitive and strategic fit, designing the solution and delivery approach, pricing and staffing the deal, routing through internal review and approval, submitting to the customer, and managing the win/loss notification. Also classified Knowledge-dominant: profitability analysis, solution design, and competitive synthesis are the bulk of the work, with pricing/approval/submission as supporting sub-activities rather than separate processes. L2O aggregate at M3: 62.5% Human / 37.5% AI — both processes stay Knowledge-dominant because lead qualification and solution/proposal synthesis remain human-centered judgment work; AI augmentation is concentrated in research synthesis, drafting support, and pipeline/CRM data mechanics rather than in the core judgment calls.
