HITL Design Insight — R2R

6 Transaction steps (35% Human at M3) — journal entries, intercompany transactions, fixed assets, and payment posting — are the AI absorption opportunity. 5 Decision gates (80% Human) carry financial authority — period-end close, account reconciliation, and period lock are irreducibly human. 4 Knowledge steps (65% Human) — external reporting, variance analysis, and FP&A — represent the AI-augmented layer. R2R aggregate at M3: 59% Human / 41% AI.
